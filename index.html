About Us

Welcome to Campus Craze - The ultimate platform for college students seeking connection, collaboration, and creativity! Here, students unite to explore shared interests, ignite innovative projects, and forge lasting connections beyond campus life.


Get a huge User base by shocasing your Projects to recruters and other students on our Community Page. get Updates with tech community and make projects with the peers of same interest.


But it doesn't end there – we also cater to your academic needs. From solving stationary problems to finding the perfect roommate, our features have got you covered! Whether it's through our stationary exchange section, roommate finder, or community chats, we strive to make your college life smoother.


Connect with peers in your field and unlock opportunities for personal and professional development. Join us on Campus Craze and embark on a journey where you engage with like-minded individuals and explore endless possibilities together!

Why Campus Craze ?

Stationery Sell / Exchange

Buy, sell, or exchange stationary items within the Campus Craze community. Find the perfect study materials or declutter your shelves by listing items you no longer need.

Flatmates

Looking for accommodation? Find flatmates with similar preferences and requirements through Campus Craze. Whether you're searching for a roommate for your dorm or planning to share a house off-campus, we've got you covered.

Notes Sell / Exchange

Need study materials? Find or sell class notes, textbooks, and other educational resources on Campus Craze. Save money and contribute to sustainability by reusing and recycling study materials within our community.

Blogs Page

Express yourself through blogging on Campus Craze. Share your thoughts, experiences, poetry, stories, and more with our diverse community. Engage in meaningful discussions and connect with fellow writers.

Dev Community

Join our thriving developer community to collaborate, learn, and showcase your projects. Whether you're a seasoned coder or just starting your coding journey, you'll find support, resources, and inspiration here.

Art Gallery

Showcase your creativity in our virtual art gallery. Share your paintings, crafts, photography, and other artistic creations with fellow students. Explore and appreciate the diverse talents within our community.

Let's Collaborate!

Join us in building something amazing together! At Campus Craze, we believe in the power of collaboration. Whether you're a student, a developer, an artist, or simply passionate about making a difference, there's a place for you in our community.

Contribute your ideas, skills, and creativity to help us create a vibrant and inclusive platform for students worldwide. Together, we can innovate, inspire, and empower each other to reach new heights.

Ready to get involved? Explore our various projects, join discussions, or start your own initiatives. Let's make a positive impact together!

Together, we can build a community where everyone has the opportunity to learn, grow, and succeed. Join Campus Craze today and be part of something extraordinary!
